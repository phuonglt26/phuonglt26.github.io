About Me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Singapore Management University, working under the supervision of Prof. Hady W. Lauw and as a member of Preferred.AI. My research focuses on explainable and trustworthy AI for recommendation systems, with interests in model attribution, large language models, and comparative opinion mining.

Research Interests

  • Trustworthy and Explainable AI (XAI)
  • Recommendation systems (Recsys)
  • Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Comparative Opinion Mining, Opinion Mining (OM)

Building AI systems that are not only accurate, but also reliable, interpretable, and grounded in meaningful reasoning.
