Profile Overview

I am now a PhD student in Computer Science at Singapore Management University, working under the supervision of Prof. Hady W. Lauw and as a member of Preferred.AI on explainable and trustworthy AI for recommendation systems. My recent work focuses on model attribution, comparative opinion mining, and language-based reasoning for reliable AI systems. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Vietnam National University, Hanoi. During my undergraduate years, I was involved in research at KTlab under the guidance of Prof. Hoang-Quynh Le, which helped shape my early interest in research. My journey began with hands-on software and data work, then developed through teaching and research, eventually leading to my current path as an AI Researcher and PhD student.
